Love and Lover - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

LOVE AND LOVER

When love is real, it finds a way!
Memorize the pathways, keep following your heart, no matter where it takes you!
Love and Lover is an endless puzzle Android game which I made it with Unity in 2021. I got the main idea from HeartBound game, remade, and add some features to it.
SWIPE to move the player and just MEMORIZE the patterns.
