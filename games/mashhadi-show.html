Mashhadi Show - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

MASHHADI SHOW

Turn on your GPS, Find the locations and solve the puzzles! Take your time and move at your own pace. You can pause and continue anytime.
Mashhadi Show is a Real-World adventure mobile game that I made with Unity in 2022. It’s a location-based game that uses mobile GPS to locate pavilions in Mashhad city and solve puzzles.
TURN-ON GPS, grab your phone and you’re good to go. Make sure to have a fully charged battery as you need your phone to NAVIGATE.
