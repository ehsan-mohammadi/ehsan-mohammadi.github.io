Knife Hit (Yalda 1400) - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

KNIFE HIT (YALDA 1400)

Throw the knives into the melones!
Be careful to not hit the knives, target carefully and become the knife master.
Knife Hit (Yalda 1400) is a remake version of Knife Hit that I made with Unity WebGL for one of the Iranian e-learning websites, Daneshjooyar, in 2021. Users could play the game on the website and by getting scores they get a discount.
Just CLICK and throw the knives into the melones.
