Ribbo - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

RIBBO

Save our space-hero from the rabits!
Fight the rabbits, destroy the asteroids, Do boss fight, and get prizes!
Ribbo is an endless shoot 'em up Android game which I made it with Unity in 2012. I got the idea from Chicken Invaders game. The main theme of the game is a battle between a lone combat spacecraft and a technologically advanced race of space-faring rabbits, who are intent on destroying him. The game is in the form of a parody of Star Wars!
Use mobile ACCELERATION-SENSOR to move spacecraft in LEFT/RIGHT directions and TOUCH to SHOOT.
