What the Bug! - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

WHAT THE BUG!

Bugs are not always bad! Sometimes they can make a hard game, easier!
Help Jundo to finish the game by using bugs. Be aware of obstacles!
What the Bug! is a platforming PC/WebGL game that I made with Unity for Backtrace Game Jam #3 (hosted by Backtrace.io) during 48 hours in 2021. The theme of this jam is "It is not a bug" and What the Bug! could ranked 15th between 64 entries.
Use LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys to WALK, hold CTRL and LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys to RUN, and press SPACE to JUMP.
