Voice of War - Game by Ehsan Mohammadi

VOICE OF WAR

Are you ready to feel battle?!
OK, Let's go!
Voice of War is a multiplayer FPS shooter PC game which I made it with Unity in 2018. This is my first experience in Unity RPC technology. In this game we have 2 teams. ATTACKERS battle to perpetrate an act of attack while DEFENSERS try to prevent it.
Use WASD to move the player, LEFT-CLICK to SHOOT, and RIGHT-CLICK to AIM.
